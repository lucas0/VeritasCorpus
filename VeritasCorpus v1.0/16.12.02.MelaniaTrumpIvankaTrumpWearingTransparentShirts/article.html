In December 2016, an image purportedly showing Ivanka Trump and Melania Trump wearing tops through which you could clearly see their breasts appeared on social media, and was shared along with sociopolitical commentary:


Neither of these photographs are real; both images were doctored to make it appear as if the two women are  wearing clothing to show their breasts. The photograph on the left was actually taken in 2007 at an after-party for the Golden Globes:


Socialite Ivanka Trump arrives at the NBC/Universal Golden Globe After Party held at the Beverly Hilton on January 15, 2007 in Beverly Hills, California.

The photograph on the right was taken in January 2013 at Mar-A-Lago in Florida:
