ORIGIN

For several years an unflattering photograph of a haggard-looking Hillary Clinton in a somewhat garish floral print outfit (reproduced above) has been circulating on social media, typically accompanied by the following caption:

Hillary Clinton has demanded that this photo be removed, so what should we do? Make it go viral!

In its widely popular form, the image depicts Clinton with messy hair and a loud top. The image is indeed based on a photograph of Secretary of State Clinton taken as she deplaned in Indonesia to attend a summit meeting in July 2011:
