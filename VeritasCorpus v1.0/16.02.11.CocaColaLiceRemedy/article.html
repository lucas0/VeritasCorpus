ORIGIN

In February 2016, a meme asserting that the soft drink Coca-Cola was an effective solution for eliminating head lice began circulating on social media. Although this claim has frequently appeared in answers to lice-related questions on web sites such as MamaPedia and Budget 101 , it has not been vetted by any authoritative source.
