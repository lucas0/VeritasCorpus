ORIGIN

On 13 October 2016, the Daily Snark web site published an article reporting that New York Giants defensive end Jason Pierre-Paul had lost a hand when his Samsung Galaxy Note 7 cell phone exploded:

In a shocking turn of unfortunate events, Jason Pierre Paul has reported lost his other hand.

Sources close to the situation say that the NY Giants defensive star who had his left hand mauled in a fireworks accident on July 4th 2015, say his Galaxy Note 7 exploded on Thursday night to live stream the Thursday Night Football game.

“It is believed Mr. Pierre-Paul had the device in his right hand (obviously) while streaming the Chargers vs Broncos game with the Galaxy Note he purchased two weeks ago,” EMT first responder Thisis Satire told reporters. “His first mistake? Watching that boring ass game. Anyways, according to him the phone self ignited and shortly after exploded while still in his hand.”
