ORIGIN

A photograph purportedly showing U.S. President Barack Obama kissing UK Prime Minister David Cameron (who somewhat resembles former U.S. Vice President Al Gore) on the mouth was widely circulated on the Internet in April 2013:

However, the image originated back in May 2011 as part of a spoof article (“Barack Obama and David Cameron Kissing with Tongues”) published on the satirical ChristWire web site and is a digital manipulation produced by combining elements from a photograph of Barack Obama sharing an affectionate moment with his wife, Michelle, with one capturing the British prime minister in a similar pose with his wife, Samantha:
