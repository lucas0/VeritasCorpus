An image purportedly showing two Boston Bruins fans holding a sign claiming that they had once had oral sex with hockey player Tyler Seguin has been circulating online for several years:


While the image displayed above has been included on several “funny sport signs” list , the original photograph was digitally manipulated. The real image was taken 31 March 2012, and shows Seguin skating by two fans holding a sign with a (slightly) less crude joke written on it:
