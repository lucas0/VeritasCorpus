ORIGIN

This list of cheeky answers to dumb tourist queries has been circulated on the Internet in various forms since at least 2000. While 2006 iterations of the list positioned it as having to do with the 2010 Winter Olympics (which were held in Vancouver, British Columbia), earlier Canadian versions merely noted the questions and answers had come from “An International Tourism Website” (always unnamed)
