ORIGIN

On 8 May 2016, an article reporting that President Obama had ordered that  abortions were to be celebrated on Mother’s Day was posted on Facebook by Fox News The FB Page:

As families all across America sit down to Mother’s Day brunch, President Obama plans to make use of the popular holiday to “celebrate reproductive diversity” and “honor mothers who exercise their reproductive freedoms.”

In a ceremony Sunday morning Obama singed the ‘Patriot Freedom of Womb Liberty Act’ which would add a new holiday,the ‘National Abortion Freedom Day’ to the already established Mother’s Day holiday.

An examination of the image that appeared with the story provided more evidence that it was fake. The group pictured in the photograph is not “celebrating abortions” outside of a Planned Parenthood; rather, it shows a group of juggalos (fans of hip hop duo Insane Clown Posse) and the image has been circulating online since at least 2014:

While Fox News The FB Page is deliberately styled to visually mimic the official Fox News Facebook page, the two entities are not affiliated. Fox News The FB Page appears to be the social media presence of web site TDTAlliance.com, which appears to exist primarily to satirize actual Fox News reporting and has previously published fake stories about Carly Fiorina , Bernie Sanders , and President Obama .
