ORIGIN

On 13 September 2017, the Twitter account @historylvrsclub recirculated an old image purportedly showing actors Heath Ledger (dressed up as “the Joker” character from 2008’s The Dark Knight) and Cillian Murphy (who played “The Scarecrow” in the same film):

The photograph isn’t real, however. It’s a digital creation that was posted in 2013 to the art-sharing web site Deviant Art by the user Eri-Sadistic-Snake . The piece, titled “ Portraits of Monsters ,” is tagged “digital art” and “photo manipulation.”
