ORIGIN

Not long after Fleer released its set of 1989 baseball trading cards, collectors noticed something unusual about the card featuring Bill Ripken (Baltimore Orioles infielder and brother of his much more famous Oriole teammate, Cal Ripken, Jr. ): letters printed in black marker on the knob of his bat. The letters were quite readable upon close examination, and they clearly spelled out the phrase “F**K FACE.” This was no case of “well, it kind of looks like it might say that”; there was no doubting exactly which two words appeared on Ripken’s bat:
