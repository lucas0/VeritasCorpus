In February 2016, a fake news story claiming that singer Adele had died in a car crash started circulating on Facebook:

While the story didn’t get a lot of attention when it was originally posted, the rumor started pick up traction after it was posted in the comment section of Adele’s YouTube video for the song “ Hello “. Various misinformed internet commentators (or trolls) posted about the death hoax, which led others to believe that the singer had actually passed away:

However, there is no truth to any of these claims. Adele is not dead; while the singer has not commented specifically on her death hoax, she is still active on her social media accounts and talking about upcoming performances:
